## Projects I'm Proud Of

### [Spasht – Real-Time Speech Therapy Engine](https://github.com/amayIIp/MITWPU_Group_16)
> A low-latency iOS app that listens in real-time to detect stuttering. I engineered an on-device ML pipeline using **Dynamic Time Warping (DTW)** for text-audio alignment. High accuracy, strict privacy, and no lag—because nobody likes waiting for their therapy app to buffer.
> 
> `Swift` `On-Device ML` `Algorithm Optimization` `Speech Recognition`

### [Smart Chess Clock (ClockMate)](https://github.com/amayIIp/ClockMate)
> Built a high-performance iOS utility that converts over-the-board chess games into digital PGN notation. It uses complex state-machine logic for real-time move tracking and sub-millisecond time control. Essentially, it makes sure you don't lose on time while looking for your queen.
> 
> `Swift` `SwiftUI` `State Management`

### [Digital Twin System Architecture](https://github.com/amayIIp/digital-twin-3d)
> A scalable 3D digital twin platform simulating urban infrastructure. I architected a Flask backend to ingest massive datasets (traffic, energy, air quality) and render them on a CesiumJS 3D globe in real-time. It's like SimCity, but with actual data.
> 
> `Python` `Flask` `CesiumJS` `Data Visualization`

### [Drone Rescue System (YOLOv8 Edge ML)](https://github.com/amayIIp/drone-rescue-app)
> An Android Edge AI app for real-time human detection in critical rescue scenarios. Integrated **TensorFlow Lite** for high-throughput mobile inference and precise GPS geotagging. Because saving lives requires a bit more than a basic `print("hello world")`.
> 
> `Kotlin` `YOLOv8` `TensorFlow Lite`
